# Your Senior Coverage Audit

Source compared: `C:\Users\bryan\Downloads\Your Senior.pdf`

## Summary

The first MVP was directionally accurate but too compressed. It showed the major phases, templates, portfolio paths, and rubrics, but it did not yet use the website's biggest advantage: giving students more context, more prompts, more evidence reminders, and more step-by-step clarity than a small booklet can hold.

This pass expands the app from a phase list into a detailed student workflow.

## What Was Missing Or Too Thin

Missing from MVP:

- Follow Senior Remind.
- Check the class website.

Improvement made:

- Added this to the Start process stop and audit section.

Still needed:
