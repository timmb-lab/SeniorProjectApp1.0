# Portfolio Checklist

Book reference: Your Senior, pp. 10-11 and 17

Your portfolio is the professional story of your Senior Capstone. Choose the minimum path or maximum path and finish it carefully.

## Minimum Portfolio Path

Final Portfolio Grade: B

- [ ] Submitted through the Senior Project Website Google Form.
- [ ] Standard resume with CTE highlights.
- [ ] Project reflection.
- [ ] Titan Tenet reflection.
- [ ] Portrait of a Titan reflection.
- [ ] Copy of formal thank-you letter.

## Maximum Portfolio Path

Final Portfolio Grade: A

- [ ] Professional physical folder.
- [ ] Title page with personal brand identity.
- [ ] Enhanced resume.
- [ ] Letter of recommendation from an adult.
- [ ] Photos of project process.
- [ ] Copy of presentation.
- [ ] All required reflections.
- [ ] Best work sample from high school.
- [ ] Copy of formal thank-you letter.
