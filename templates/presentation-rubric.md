# Presentation Rubric Self-Check

Book reference: Your Senior, p. 15

Use this before Presentation Day.

## Project Overview

- Does not meet: audience does not understand the project.
- Meets: student describes the topic.
- Exceeds: student explains what they did or made in a clear and relevant way.

## Connection To Program

- Does not meet: no clear program connection.
- Meets: audience knows the program and sees a connection.
- Exceeds: connection to program and project work is clear.

## Explanation Of Final Process Or Product

- Does not meet: audience does not understand what the student did.
- Meets: audience understands the process.
- Exceeds: audience understands the process and outcome, and the final product has impact.

## Presentation Skills

- Does not meet: late, unorganized, unrehearsed, or difficult to follow.
- Meets: on time and organized.
- Exceeds: on time, organized, rehearsed, and enjoyable.
