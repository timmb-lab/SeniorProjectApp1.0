# Student Pilot Onboarding Checklist

Status: draft checklist for a future approved real-student pilot. Use only after the school approves the pilot and student data handling process.

## Login Path

- Use the sign-in path provided by your teacher or project coordinator.
- If the app asks you to reset a password, follow the school-approved reset instructions.
- If you cannot sign in, contact the named support person instead of creating another account.

## My Capstone Overview

Use My Capstone to answer: what do I do next?

- Today: check what to do next and where to click.
- My Work: see your project work, proof files, and what you turned in.
- Feedback: read teacher or mentor notes and work to fix.
- Final Checklist: check final files and closeout status if this is included in the pilot.

## Simple Daily Steps

1. Sign in.
2. Open My Capstone.
3. Start on Today.
4. Do the item marked next.
5. Open Feedback if something needs changes.
6. Ask for help if work, feedback, or proof files look wrong.

## What To Do If Something Is Missing

- If your project, phase, or requirement is missing, tell your teacher or support contact.
- If a due date or program looks wrong, do not enter duplicate work; ask staff to check your roster/program assignment.
- If you see another student's information, stop using the app and tell your teacher immediately.

## How To Read Feedback

- Look for the latest teacher decision.
- If the work needs changes, fix the item named in the feedback.
- Attach or link proof only where your teacher asks for it.
- Ask for help if the feedback does not match the work you submitted.

## How To Submit Or Upload If Supported

- Use the app's proof or submission controls only for your own project work.
- Do not upload files that include another student's private information.
- If an upload fails, save your work locally and contact the support person with the error text.
- Do not paste passwords, private links, or unrelated personal information into project proof.

## Who To Ask For Help

- Project or feedback question: Program Teacher.
- Mentor/support question: assigned mentor or teacher.
- Login/access question: support owner or project coordinator.
- Privacy or wrong-student data concern: teacher or pilot owner immediately.

## Privacy Reminder

This app is for your Senior Capstone work during an approved pilot. Use your own account, do not share login information, and report anything that looks like the wrong student's record.

Student-facing wording should follow `docs/design/plain-language-and-esl-ui-guidelines.md`.
