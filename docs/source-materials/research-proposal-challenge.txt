My  proposed  solution  to  the  problem  of  unhealthy  eating  is  to  encourage  healthier  eating  habits  by  presenting  nutritious  foods  in  an  entertaining  way.  Unhealthy  eating  is  a  major  issue  because  it  can  lead  to  long-term  health  problems,  especially  for  younger  people.  I  plan  to  do  this  by  creating  a  mod  for  the  popular  video  game  Minecraft.  This  game  is  particularly  popular  among  younger  age  groups,  who  often  struggle  the  most  with  making  healthy  food  choices.  The  mod  would  include  gameplay  elements  that  reward  players  for  choosing  healthy  foods,  making  learning  about  nutrition  more  engaging  and  fun.  I  believe  that  using  an  interactive  and  entertaining  approach  like  this  can  positively  influence  players’  real-life  food  choices  and  bring  this  target  audience  a  step  closer  to  better  overall  wellness.
